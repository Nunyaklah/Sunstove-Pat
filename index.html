THIS CHANGES EVERYTHING!

The GoSun Stove is a game-changing, fuel-free cooking technology with multiple applications to improve lives. Fast to cook, requiring only partial sunlight, and accessibly priced, the GoSun Stove is saving time, money and forest around the world. Working with the Global Alliance for Clean Cookstoves, the technology has been successfully developed and piloted in Guatemala, resulting in a desirable product with a strong potential to change lives via social enterprise.

This technology has been developed in a commercial scale in Ghana, West Africa. And stoves are now on sale to all parts of Africa

HOW IT WORKS

The core of our technology is the solar vacuum tube, a near-perfect insulator, allowing it to cook in even the most challenging conditions
